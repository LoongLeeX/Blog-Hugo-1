| Coffee Name  | Essential                                               |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Espresso     | Espresso <br>(1 shot)                                   |
| Flat White   | 2/3 Steamed Milk <br>1/3 Espresso                       |
| Cappuccino   | 1/3 Milk foam <br>1/3 Hot Milk <br>1/3 Espresso         |
| Americano    | 2/3 Water <br>1/3 Espresso                              |
| Latte        | Milk foam <br>1/3 Steamed Milk <br>1/3 Espresso         |
| Mocha        | 1/5 Steamed Milk <br>2/5 Chocolate <br>2/5 Espresso     |
| Affogato     | 2/5 Vanilla Ice Cream <br>2/5 Espresso                  |
| Macchiato    | Dash of Steamed Milk <br>Double shot Espresso           |
| Irish Coffee | 1/2 Whipped Milk <br>1/2 Irish Whisky <br>1/2 Espresso  |
| Marocchino   | 1/2 Whipped Milk <br>2/5 Hot Chocolate <br>1/2 Espresso |
| Lungo        | Double shot Espresso                                    |
| Con Panna    | 2/3 Whipped Cream <br>1/3 Espresso                      |
